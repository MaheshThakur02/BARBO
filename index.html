About Us

Welcome to BARBO – Revolutionizing Your Barber Experience

At BARBO, we believe your time is valuable, and waiting in line for a haircut shouldn’t be part of your day. That’s why we created an innovative solution that eliminates the hassle of long waits at the barbershop. Our platform allows you to book slots at your favorite barbershop in advance, giving you the freedom to plan your visit at a time that suits you best.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to transform the traditional barbershop experience by reducing wait times and bringing convenience to your grooming routine. With BARBO, you can enjoy a smooth, seamless booking process and more time doing what matters most.

Why BARBO?

  • No More Waiting: Book your slot and avoid the queues. Show up when it’s your turn, and enjoy a hassle-free visit.
  • Real-Time Availability: See up-to-date schedules of your preferred barbers and choose the time that works for you.
  • Trusted Barbershops: We partner with only the best barbershops to ensure you get the quality and service you deserve.

Our Story

BARBO was founded with a vision to solve one of the most frustrating parts of visiting the barbershop – the wait. Whether it’s an early morning rush or a weekend jam, we’ve all been there. Our founders and co-founders, Himanshu Tiwari Mahesh Patap Singh Tarun pratap singh, set out to change that. With a background in technology and a love for efficiency, BARBO was born to give customers more control over their time.

How It Works

Using BARBO is easy. Simply browse our list of partner barbershops, choose your barber and time slot, and confirm your booking. It’s that simple! Whether you're planning your day or squeezing in a haircut between meetings, we’ve got you covered.

Our Values

  • Convenience: We prioritize ease of use to ensure every customer gets their haircut without the hassle of waiting.
  • Quality: Our partner barbershops are vetted for their high standards, so you know you're getting the best.
  • Time-Saving: We understand the value of your time, and our goal is to make every visit efficient and enjoyable.
